# **Genealyze: A Simple Tool for DNA Sequence Analysis**

**Genealyze** is a Python library designed to make working with DNA, RNA, and protein sequences easy and straightforward. With its beginner-friendly interface and essential features, Genealyze is perfect for those just starting to explore molecular biology.

---

## **Key Features**
- **DNA and RNA Analysis**  
  Quickly identify nucleotides and convert between DNA and RNA sequences.

- **Sequence Comparison**  
  Detect similarities or differences between biological sequences.

- **Sequence Manipulation**  
  Replace, insert, or simulate mutations on DNA sequences.

---

## **Applications**
- **Learning**  
  Ideal for students looking to understand the structure and function of DNA.

- **Basic Research**  
  Supports simple tasks in biological analysis.

---

**Genealyze** is simple, yet powerful enough to handle basic bioinformatics needs.
